.Land Use and Environmental Modeling


The projects were completed under the guidance of the course, "Land Use and Environmental Modeling" This course aimed to introduce students to various GIS-based land use and environmental planning models, including TR55, BASINS, HAZUS, UPlan, CUF/CURBA, CommunityViz, and other packages, to enable students to analyze existing patterns, identify and parameterize key trends and urban processes, visualize alternative futures, and evaluate development impacts. The course focused on the use of spatial data and models to provide insights into complex relationships between land use, environment, and development, and to inform more effective and sustainable planning decisions.
